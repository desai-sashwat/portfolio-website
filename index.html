About Me

I'm a graduate student pursuing my MS in Applied Mathematics at Northeastern University, where I combine rigorous mathematical theory with cutting-edge machine learning applications.

My research interests span mathematical economics, medical imaging, and data-driven modeling. I'm passionate about leveraging mathematical frameworks to solve complex real-world problems, from healthcare diagnostics to economic forecasting.

With experience as a Graduate Teaching Assistant and research collaborations with Harvard-founded companies like Zeta Surgical, I bridge the gap between academic rigor and practical implementation in data science and machine learning.
